About Me

My name is Miller Boyd, and I have coding for the past 5 years. I started in High School taking Java classes that I excelled in and thoroughly enjoyed. After I finished the AP test with a perfect score and led software development on my robotics team, I finally decided to pursue Computer Science in college. I went to the University of Texas at Arlington for my first year and a half of college, until I ultimately felt like it was not the school for me. I would transfer to Blinn college and continue to learn C++. At this point, I had a great grasp of back-end development, but that was not enough.

I am now on my way to becoming a Web Developer. I enjoy all facets of programming, so I have enrolled in a boot camp to enhance my knowledge further. I have so far learned a lot about HTML and CSS and feel very comfortable in my efforts with both. Thank you for browsing my portfolio, I hope you enjoy my work.
